Behind the Code & Cause

A versatile professional with strengths in web development, environmental education, and administrative operations. I bring a well-rounded approach that combines technical proficiency, mission-driven advocacy, and organized business support.

Through SheCodes, I built a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and API integration—designing responsive tools that prioritize user experience. My background in administrative support includes roles such as Associate Billing Representative Clerk and Accounts Receivable Clerk, where I handled billing processes, account resolution, and client/vendor communication.

I’m also a certified Climate Reality Leader, actively working with township schools to promote sustainability and climate action. I completed the Grant Writing Workshop offered by Leaders of Africa Institute and have been accepted into their main program (not yet commenced), reflecting my ongoing investment in capacity building and project impact.

Additional certifications include the Project Management Certificate and participation in a business incubation program with RaizeCorp, further enhancing my strategic and operational capabilities.

At the intersection of technology, environmental advocacy, and structured administration, I am committed to creating work that is impactful, well-organized, and aligned with clear purpose.
